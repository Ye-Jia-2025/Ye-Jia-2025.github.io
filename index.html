Biography

Ye Jia, IEEE Member, is a professor and doctoral supervisor at the School of Electrical Engineering, Chongqing University. Her research focuses on wireless communication system design, analysis, and optimization, as well as the intersection of communications and power systems. She has published over 40 research papers in related fields.

In recent years, she has led projects such as the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) Young Scientists Fund and the Chongqing Natural Science Foundation Innovation and Development Joint Fund. She has also participated in projects such as the National Key R&D Program and the National Natural Science Foundation of China General Program. In 2022, she received the IEEE Andrea Goldsmith Young Scholars Award.

She is currently an Associate Editor for IEEE Communications Letters. She is a recipient of the National Excellent Young Scholars Fund (Overseas), the National Natural Science Foundation of China Excellent Young Scholars Fund (Overseas), and the Chongqing Talents-Young Top Talents Fund.
